03 WHY FLEET WRAPS

Mobile Marketing

Your vehicles are already on the road — make every mile count for your brand.

01

Brand Visibility

A single wrapped vehicle generates 30,000–70,000 daily impressions. Your brand is seen by thousands of potential customers every day.

02

Paint Protection

Vinyl wraps act as a protective layer, shielding your fleet's paint from road debris, sun damage, and minor scratches — preserving resale value.

03

Targeted Reach

Unlike static ads, fleet wraps reach people in your exact service area. Your wrapped vehicles work where your customers live and work.

04

Lowest Cost Per Impression

At $0.04 per 1,000 impressions, fleet wraps are the most cost-effective form of advertising — outperforming billboards, radio, and digital ads.

05

Professional Image

A uniformly branded fleet projects professionalism and trustworthiness. Customers are 15x more likely to remember your brand from a vehicle wrap.

06

Non-Stop Advertising

Fleet wraps work 24/7 — in traffic, parked at job sites, or in your driveway. No recurring monthly costs like traditional advertising methods.
